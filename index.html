In the spirit of our namesake, the Starnosedmole token employs a novel Sensory Transaction Algorithm (STA). This feature allows SNM tokens to "sense" large transaction activities and automatically adjust fees for enhanced liquidity and reduced market manipulation.

Hybrid Utility-Store of Value Design

SNM token is neither just a utility token nor solely a store of value; it's a hybrid. This allows it to serve functional roles in various decentralized apps (dApps) while also acting as a hedge against market volatility, providing long-term investors with the best of both worlds.

Community governance is at the heart of the Starnosedmole ecosystem. Token holders have the power to propose, discuss, and vote on network changes, feature implementations, and expansion plans. Our decentralized governance model ensures that no single entity controls the network, fostering a genuinely democratic digital asset.

In the new era of tech.

Time-Locked Smart Contracts

SNM will incorporate time-locked smart contracts for fund allocations and protocol changes. This means that any changes to the network's code, including those dictated by community governance, undergo a mandatory "cooling-off" period. This time-lock allows the community to review and potentially veto unintended or malicious changes before they go live.

Multi-Signature Wallets for Core Operations

SNM uses multi-signature (multi-sig) wallets for core operations such as fund management and contract upgrades. This requirement means that no single entity can make decisions; instead, a quorum of authorized signers (e.g., team leads and community-elected representatives) must approve every high-stakes transaction. This feature significantly reduces the risk.
